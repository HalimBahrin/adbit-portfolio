01 // IDENTITY

Profile

Since 2012.

Based in Brunei Darussalam.

As a dedicated music producer with 13 years of experience, I have built a reputation in Brunei’s local music scene since 2012 while expanding internationally—specifically Malaysia and Singapore.

My work spans electronic genres like Progressive House, Electro House, Future Bass, and experimental fusion. I bridge the gap between technical audio engineering and creative composition.

Currently focusing on sound design, remixes, and original releases that emphasize uniqueness and cultural influence.

Achievements

WARNER MUSIC GROUP
Released the first EDM album in Brunei with "The Brotherhood" under Warner Music Group’s Level Music.
PROGRESIF MEDIA
Featured on Local EDM Album Vol.6 with Shasha for the song "Ascend (Wait For You)".
VIRAL REMIXES
Remixed SWANZ & ASMAI song "MAJAL", contributing to 15k listeners for the local electronic movement in Brune and also Suasana Hari Raya which garnered 400k+ views.
ROYAL BRUNEI AIRLINES
Featured in Royal Brunei Airlines partnership with KYSB show, FLIGHT CLVB.
KRISTAL FM
Performed a 3 sessions mixshow with KYSB Radio Show under KristalFM with the alias Bass Howlers and also TWONATURE.
MEDIA FEATURES
Got featured on Electronic Sound Magazine Malaysia and also on a few songs played on the Malaysian radio.
COLLABORATIONS
