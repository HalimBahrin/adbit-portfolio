07 // INFO

FAQ

How do I send my files for mixing/mastering?
Please upload your stems (WAV 24bit/44.1kHz or higher) to Google Drive, Dropbox, or WeTransfer. Ensure all effects on the master bus are turned off before exporting unless they are creative effects essential to the sound.
What is your typical turnaround time?
For mastering, it usually takes 5 days max. For mixing and full production, it can take 1-2 weeks depending on the complexity of the project and current workload. Rush delivery is available for an extra fee.
Do you offer revisions?
Yes, I want you to be 100% happy with the result. Mixing and Production packages include up to 3 major revisions. Mastering includes 2 revisions. Additional revisions can be discussed.
What payment methods do you accept?
I accept Bank Transfer (BIBD/Baiduri) for local clients in Brunei. For international clients, I accept PayPal. A 50% deposit is required to start the project.
